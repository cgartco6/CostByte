What is CostByte?

CostByte is a real-time food cost tracking and kitchen intelligence platform designed to help hotel operators identify wastage, monitor price fluctuations, and control costs with precision.

Real-time Food Cost Tracking

See how your food cost is performing across locations daily, not just at month-end.

Price Change Alerts

Be instantly alerted when supplier prices increase — no more surprises on your P&L.

Smart Purchasing Insights

Identify lower-cost suppliers or opportunities to consolidate orders.

Waste Monitoring

Spot where and why waste is happening and reduce unnecessary losses.

Inventory Automation

Save hours on manual stock counts with mobile-based inventory tools.
